## 1. The Service and What EVA Is (and Is Not)

### 1.1 What EVA provides

EVA is a **peer-to-peer marketplace platform** that enables:

- **Drivers** to discover, search, filter, book and pay for access to EV charging facilities listed by hosts;
- **Hosts** to list chargers, set pricing and availability, manage bookings, communicate with drivers and receive payouts;
- **Both parties** to use tools such as maps, navigation, messaging, reviews, notifications, optional marketplace listings, and an in-app AI assistant.

### 1.2 What EVA does not provide

Unless expressly stated in writing:

- EVA is **not** an electricity supplier, energy broker, utility, distribution network operator, or reseller of electricity;
- EVA does **not** buy, sell, generate, transmit, distribute or resell energy;
- EVA does **not** own, operate, install, maintain, certify or inspect hosts' chargers, electrical installations, parking areas or premises;
- EVA does **not** guarantee the availability, safety, legality, suitability, accuracy or performance of any charger, host, driver, route, price estimate or third-party service.

**Electricity** consumed during a session is supplied through the host's charger and the host's arrangements with their energy supplier and property. EVA provides **software, booking, payment facilitation, communication and support tools only**.

### 1.3 Contracting parties

When you book as a driver, your **charging access arrangement** is with the **host** (and your **platform use** is with EVA). EVA is not a party to the host's supply of electricity or physical access except as platform operator.
